Google Calendar Embed
Shows your Google Calendar inside Norx AI on the Calendar page

1. Open Google Calendar → Settings → Settings for my calendars
2. Click your calendar → scroll to "Integrate calendar"
3. Copy the Embed code — paste just the src="..." URL below

Data Management & Migration
Export, import, or migrate your full data between app versions or URLs
Moved to a new Netlify URL? Your contacts and conversations are stored in the browser for your old URL.
1. Open your old Netlify URL → Settings → click "Export Full Backup"
2. Come back to this new URL → click "Import Full Backup" → select the file
All contacts, conversations, campaigns, stats and templates will be restored.
