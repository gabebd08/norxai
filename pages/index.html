FAQ
Common questions
Do I need a Twilio account?
Yes — Twilio is how the actual SMS messages get sent. You sign up at twilio.com for free, add a small balance (~$20), and paste your credentials into Norx AI. SMS costs about $0.008 per message — 1,000 texts costs ~$8.
Is cold SMS legal?
Cold SMS outreach to businesses is common and widely practiced. We recommend always including an opt-out option ("Reply STOP") which Norx AI adds automatically to every message.
Where do the phone numbers come from?
The Lead Finder pulls data directly from Google's Places API — the same numbers that appear on Google Maps and Google Business Profiles. These are publicly listed business phone numbers.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings at any time and you won't be charged again.
How is this different from GoHighLevel?
GHL costs $297–$497/month and is built for full agency management. Norx AI is purpose-built for cold outreach and lead generation — faster, simpler, and a fraction of the price.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — every plan includes a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You only get charged after the trial ends if you decide to continue.
